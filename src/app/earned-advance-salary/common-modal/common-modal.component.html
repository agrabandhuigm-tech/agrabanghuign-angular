Quick Info

The Pension advance is based on the amount you have earned so far.

For instance:

  • Say your net pension is Rs 40,000.
  • You apply on the 14th day of the month, but you have taken 2 days unpaid leave.
  • Also, you have already taken 2 days of pension in advance this month.

You can receive an advance equivalent to 10 days worth of your net pension.

To receive your earned salary in advance, you need to submit an undertaking.

This undertaking will share your information with the service provider you choose.
