Frequently Asked Questions

About Refyne
Q.1. What is Refyne? Refyne is Asia's #1 holistic Financial Wellness Suite. The company offers advance against 3-month pension service, earned pension advance drawal access scheme, and other financial wellness products and services. Your company has partnered with Refyne to give you access to some or all Refyne products.
Q.2. How does Refyne function? Refyne integrates with the government of Rajasthan's payroll/pension management systems. Once the government uploads your pension data, you can see what part of your pension amount you have access to. When you make a withdrawal on Refyne, you don't have to make any repayment—your company will deduct the amount from your monthly pension and pay to Refyne.
Q.3. How can I use Refyne? You can log in to iFMS3.0 through your SSO ID and submit your consent and undertaking to the service provider or financial institution authorized by RFSDL through the Pensioners/Family Pensioners Self-Service. You can access Refyne via IOS/Android App or Web App.
Q.4. How safe is my data with Refyne? Refyne stores all its data securely behind a firewall, which can only be accessed by parties with clear permission from the user. We are ISO 27001 and SOC2 (type-2) certified and follow the Reserve Bank of India's (RBI) guidelines.
About KYC Verification
Q.1. Is it compulsory to complete KYC? According to Reserve Bank of India's (RBI) guidelines, KYC is mandatory for banks or NBFCs to transfer any amount to a customer's account. This is a one-time process, and it takes less than two minutes to upload the documents directly to the Refyne app.
Q.2. How long does KYC verification take? The verification process is instant, but in some cases, it may take up to two hours.
Q.3. What are the documents required for verification? We suggest you use Aadhaar-based authentication. However, your mobile number should be linked with your Aadhaar for that. Refyne also accepts Driver's Licence and Voter's ID.
Q.4. I've received an email saying my KYC is incomplete. What should I do? Sometimes KYC verification fails for the following reasons:
● Uploaded selfie doesn't match with the image on your documents.
● Your PAN details have not been uploaded in the correct format.
● There are discrepancies with your address proof.
● There's a mismatch between your bank and personal details.
If the issue persists, please reach out to your HR.
Q.5. I got Re.1 in my account. Why did I get that? When you update your bank details, we deposit Re.1 to authenticate the account. This helps us confirm that the account belongs to a registered employee.
About earned pension advance drawal access scheme
Q.1. Why is my earned pension advance amount different from my actual pension amount? Your earned pension amount is calculated based on the pension details provided by your employer. It may differ from your actual monthly pension.
Q.2. How is my daily earned pension amount calculated? We divide your pension pay by the number of days in the month. This gives us your daily pension.
Q.3. Do I have to pay to check my earned pension amount on Refyne? No, tracking your earned pension amount is entirely free on Refyne.
About Pension Withdrawals
Q.1. Why am I unable to make withdrawals? Transactions can be paused for many reasons: ● We may not have received the money you withdrew in the previous month. ● Your pension amount is being calculated by your employer and your withdrawals may be paused for some time.
Q.2. Why hasn't my withdrawn money been credited? We transfer the amount immediately, but your bank may take up to 5 business days to deposit it into your account. Contact us at support@refyne.co.in or +91 8064899999 if it has been longer.
Account Settings
Q.1. How do I update my phone number? Send your old and new mobile numbers, together with your employee code, to support@refyne.co.in. We'll update your phone number once your employer confirms it, which could take up to 3 days.
Q.2. How do I update my bank account on the Refyne app? 1. Open the Refyne app.
2. At the bottom of the screen, tap the 'Profile' tab.
3. Scroll down to the 'Bank Details' section.
4. Select 'Edit' from the drop-down menu.
5. Enter your new account number or IFSC code.
6. Click 'Update,' and the new bank account will be verified in about 10 minutes.
Note: You can only add your own bank accounts. Accounts of friends and family members cannot be added.
Q.3. How do I deactivate my account / delete all my data on the Refyne App? We are sorry to see you leave. We are always ready to address your concerns. You can reach out to Customer Support for any issues you might be facing. Please be informed that you will not be able to log in to the platform and use any refyne services once your account is deleted.
● You can always choose to reactivate your account by reaching out to our Customer Support team but you will have to start again as a new user.
Q.4. Why did I get less money than I had requested? ● We deduct a nominal processing fee for every transaction and deposit the balance amount into your bank account. The deduction break-up will be sent to your registered email and updated on the Refyne app.
